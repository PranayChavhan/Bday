In the dreamy garden where moonlight glows

I found a love that endlessly grows

Eyes shining bright like stars in the night

Your smile brings warmth, pure and light

Your laughter, a melody in the air

In love's song, our hearts together dare

Though we may fight, in storms we'll weather

Promise to stay together, always and forever

With love on your 21st, a wish so clear

Happy Birthday, my dear, cherish this year.
